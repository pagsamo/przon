FREE WORLDWIDE SHIPPING - NORMAL MAIL

Estimated shipping time for this service is about 3-6 weeks with no tracking number and is applicable for orders less than $100.

Customers who need their orders urgently, please purchase over the minimum values for Express shipping. Otherwise, please contact us to pay for Express service.

FREE WORLDWIDE SHIPPING - EXPRESS MAIL

Orders with minimum value $100 shipping to most parts of the world will be upgraded automatically to Express services.

Whereas for countries such as Latvia, Lithuania, Reunion Island, Israel, UAE, Turkey, All middle east countries, All Africa countries, Brazil, Mauritius, Guam, Fiji, Bulgaria, Saudi Arabia and all of South America, a minimum order value of $200 is required to be eligible for Express services.

SHIPPING TO AUSTRALIA

Orders with minimum value of $100 will be shipped via Express services to most major cities. (e.g Sydney, Melbourne)

Orders to remote cities / areas will be shipped via registered airmail instead, with estimated 1 – 2 weeks of shipping time. Full detailed tracking will be provided.

Customers with any doubts may feel free to contact us to check which service their order will be shipped with. (Please include your full address in your inquiry for faster and more accurate advice)

Due to Russian Customs regulations, no express shipping services are allowed. All orders will be shipped via registered airmail services. Shipping time is around 6 – 8 weeks.

As a result of this regulations, we regret to state that we are unable to ship any fishing rods to Russia.
